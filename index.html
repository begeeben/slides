What is not supported:

  • Play multiple media files simultaneously on iOS (Android unknown)
  • Fast/slow motion on iOS (Android unknown)
  • Play video inline on small screen iOS:
      Video will be forced to play in fullscreen mode
  • Post effects not available for embeded youtube player
